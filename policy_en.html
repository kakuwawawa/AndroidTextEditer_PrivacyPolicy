1. Information handled by the App

The App does not send or collect personal information that identifies users, such as names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, account information, location information, device identifiers, or advertising IDs, to the developer’s server.

For the functionality of the App, the following information may be handled locally on the user’s device:

  • Text files and folders created or edited by users within the App
  • Text shared by users from other apps
  • Content, file names, and URIs of text files opened through file selection or sharing operations
  • Unsaved editing content, search strings, and character encoding settings
  • Commands entered in the session feature, working directories, command outputs, and pane names

This information is used to provide App features such as editing, saving, displaying, searching, character encoding conversion, exporting, and session display.

2. Where information is stored

Files created in the App are generally stored in the App-specific storage area on the user’s device.

When a user opens an external file, the App reads and writes that file only within the scope permitted by Android. When a user performs an export operation, the App writes text to the location selected through Android’s save destination selection screen.

The App does not request internet access permission and does not send text or file contents handled within the App to the developer’s server or to third-party servers.

If Android or Google backup features are enabled in the device settings, App data may be included in backups on the device or in the user’s Google Account. This is a function of the operating system and is not information collected by the developer.

3. Provision to third parties

Because the developer does not collect user information through the App, the App does not provide such information to third parties.

However, if the user uses Android’s sharing feature, file save destination selection, or integration with other apps, data may be passed to the selected app or service. In such cases, the handling of that data is subject to the privacy policy of each app or service.

4. External services, analytics, advertising, and in-app purchases

The App does not use advertising services, analytics services, crash analytics services, or login services.

In the future, the App may use Google Play’s in-app purchase feature to provide additional features. When in-app purchases are used, payment processing is handled by Google Play, and the developer does not obtain payment information such as credit card numbers, bank account information, or Google Account passwords.

To provide in-app purchases, confirm purchase status, enable purchased features, prevent unauthorized use, and restore purchase history, the App may handle purchase status, product IDs, order-related identifiers, and similar information provided by Google Play within the App.

For information handling by Google Play, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy and the Google Play Terms of Service.

5. Session feature

The App’s session feature displays and retains commands entered by users and their outputs on the screen. Command execution results may include user input or information related to files on the device.

Command contents and outputs are not sent to the developer. Users should be careful when sharing, exporting, or displaying commands or outputs that contain confidential information.

6. User control

Users can delete files and folders created within the App using the App’s delete feature or through the device’s app settings.

When the App is uninstalled, data stored in the App-specific storage area is normally deleted from the device. However, files exported by the user to external storage locations, files managed by other apps, or data included in operating system backups may remain after uninstallation.

7. Children’s privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Policy may be revised as necessary due to changes in laws and regulations, additions or changes to App features, or other reasons. If there are important changes, they will be announced on the App distribution page or in this file.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding privacy in this App, please contact the developer using the contact information listed on the App distribution page.
